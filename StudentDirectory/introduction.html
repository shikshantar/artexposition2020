The Art Portfolio

    STILL LIFE
    In art paper one, students are required to sketch or paint a group of objects which will be
    artificial or natural
    and may include things such as cut flowers, fruits, vegetables, a growing plant, as well as
    domestic or other artificial objects. still life paintings show us a new way of looking at the
    ordinary objects around us and find the beauty in mundane objects.
    
    
    Applied art
    In applied art, students learn to design book covers, posters, invitation and greeting cards,
    repeat patterns and more. It is a broad subject area that combines aesthetics, design, consumer
    need, and finding practical solutions to problems.
    
    
    Crafts
    Students are required to submit craft work for projects. They can be earthenware, abstract or
    figurative sculpture, cloth or rug design, mosaic, embroidery, collage or decorative panels, prints
    made with wood or lino blocks, photography and many more.
